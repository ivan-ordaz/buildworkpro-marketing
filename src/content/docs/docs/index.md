Welcome to the BuildWorkPro documentation. Here you will find everything you need to manage your construction business, from creating your first bid to tracking pay applications.

## Where to start

- **New to BuildWorkPro?** Begin with the [Introduction](/docs/getting-started/introduction) and [Quick Start](/docs/getting-started/quick-start) guides.
- **Ready to bid?** Learn how to [create a bid](/docs/bids/creating-a-bid) and [send it to a client](/docs/bids/sending).
- **Managing a project?** Explore [project views](/docs/projects/overview), [phases and tasks](/docs/projects/phases-tasks), and the [Gantt chart](/docs/projects/gantt).
- **Getting paid?** Set up [pay applications](/docs/pay-apps/creating) and track them through [approval](/docs/pay-apps/approval-workflow).

## Core modules

- **Bids** -- Build detailed estimates with line items, markup, and labor rates.
- **Projects** -- Plan work with phases, tasks, and visual scheduling.
- **Pay Apps** -- Submit AIA-style payment applications and track approvals.
- **Change Orders** -- Document scope changes and their contract impact.
- **Field** -- Log daily activity, weather, photos, and crew hours.
- **CRM** -- Manage contacts, leads, and your sales pipeline.

Use the sidebar to navigate through each section.
