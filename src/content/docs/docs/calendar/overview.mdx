The **Calendar** module gathers dates from across BuildWorkPro into one view. Open it from **Calendar** in the sidebar. It is read-mostly: you browse what is coming up and reschedule tasks by dragging.

![The Calendar in month view showing project, phase, and lead events with the color legend](/docs-screenshots/calendar/calendar-month.png)

## What appears on the calendar

The calendar aggregates six kinds of events, all on by default:

- **Tasks** -- task due dates.
- **Phases** -- phase dates.
- **Projects** -- project dates.
- **Leads** -- lead follow-ups and expected close dates.
- **Activities** -- logged activities.
- **Bids** -- bid due and valid-until dates.

A color legend maps each type, and you can turn types off in the filters.

## Views and navigation

Switch between **Month**, **Week**, **Day**, and **Agenda** views. Use **Today** and the arrows to move through time.

## Filter events

Open **Filters** to narrow the calendar by **Event Types**, **Assigned To**, and **Project**.

## Reschedule tasks

Drag a task to another day to change its due date. Other event types are read-only on the calendar -- edit them on their own records.

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** All roles can view the calendar. Creating an event with **Create Event** requires Member or above, so Field Crew and Viewer cannot add events.
</Aside>
