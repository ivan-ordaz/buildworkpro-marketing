Every project has three collaboration tabs -- **Documents**, **Comments**, and **Activity** -- that keep files, discussion, and history together with the work.

![A project's Documents tab listing an attached contract and drawing with preview and download actions](/docs-screenshots/projects/project-documents.png)

## Documents

The **Documents** tab lists files attached to the project. Upload contracts, drawings, permits, and photos here, and they stay linked to the project. Each file can be previewed, downloaded, or removed.

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Company Admin, Manager, or Member to upload. Company Admin or Manager to delete. Field Crew and Viewer can view and download files but cannot upload or delete them.
</Aside>

Files attached here also appear in the app-wide [Documents library](/docs/documents/library/), where you can browse files across every project, bid, and contact.

## Comments

The **Comments** tab is a threaded discussion for the project. Post an update, reply to a colleague, and mention a teammate to notify them.

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Company Admin, Manager, or Member to comment. Field Crew and Viewer can read comments but cannot post.
</Aside>

## Activity

The **Activity** tab is a read-only history of everything that has happened on the project -- status changes, edits, new tasks, uploaded files, and more. Use it to see who did what and when. All roles can view the activity feed.
