The Gantt chart shows a project's phases and tasks as horizontal bars on a timeline, so you can see how work overlaps and reschedule it by dragging. It is a view of the **Phases & Tasks** tab, not a separate tab.

## Open the Gantt view

<Steps>

1. **Open Phases & Tasks.** Open the project and click the **Phases & Tasks** tab.

2. **Switch to Gantt.** In the tab header, click **Gantt**. Click **List** to switch back.

</Steps>

Only tasks with dates appear on the timeline. Set a **Start Date** and **Duration** on a task -- see [phases and tasks](/docs/projects/phases-tasks/) -- to place it on the chart.

## Read the timeline

- Each task is a bar; phases roll up into a summary bar above their tasks.
- A red vertical line marks **today**. Click **Today** in the toolbar to scroll back to it.
- Dependency arrows connect tasks that depend on one another.
- The legend at the bottom maps bar colors to **To Do**, **In Progress**, and **Done**.

## Change the zoom

Use the zoom buttons to fit more or less of the schedule on screen. Three levels are available -- **day**, **week**, and **month** -- with the current level shown between the buttons. Day view shades weekends.

## Reschedule by dragging

<Steps>

1. **Move a task.** Drag a bar left or right to shift its start and end dates together.

2. **Change its length.** Drag the right edge of a bar to extend or shorten it; this updates the end date and duration.

3. **Edit details.** Hover a bar and click the pencil to open the task dialog.

</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Company Admin, Manager, or Member. Field Crew and Viewer can view the Gantt chart but cannot drag tasks to reschedule them.
</Aside>
