Pay applications and change orders both render to a PDF and can be emailed to your customer with the PDF attached. The flow is the same for each.

![The pay app PDF preview dialog showing the rendered application with Open and Download buttons](/docs-screenshots/pay-apps/pay-app-pdf-preview.png)

## Preview and download a PDF

On a pay app or change order detail page:

- Click **Preview** to open the PDF in a dialog. From there you can **Open** it in a new tab or **Download** it.
- Click **Download PDF** to save it directly. Files are named after the document, for example `PayApp-P-1024-PA001.pdf` or `ChangeOrder-CO-001.pdf`.

## Email it to your customer

<Steps>

1. **Open the send dialog.** Click **Send to Customer**.

2. **Choose a template.** Pick an **Email Template** to prefill the subject and body, or write your own.

3. **Set recipients.** Fill in **To**, and add **Cc** or **Bcc** as needed.

4. **Send.** Review the message and send. The PDF attaches automatically.

</Steps>

The email body supports merge fields -- such as the document number, project name, and amount -- so a template fills in each customer's details.

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Company Admin or Manager to send. Email templates are managed by the same roles under Settings.
</Aside>

<Aside type="caution">
  Large attachments can exceed your mail provider's limit. BuildWorkPro warns you when an attachment approaches the 25 MB Gmail ceiling.
</Aside>
