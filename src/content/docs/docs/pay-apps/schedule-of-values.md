The schedule of values (SOV) is the backbone of your pay app. It lists every billable item on the project and tracks how much work has been completed.

## SOV structure

Each line in the SOV includes:

- **Item number** -- Sequential identifier.
- **Description of work** -- What the line item covers (e.g., "Rough-In Electrical - 2nd Floor").
- **Scheduled value** -- The total contract amount for this item.
- **Previous applications** -- How much was billed in prior pay apps.
- **This period** -- The amount you are billing now.
- **Materials stored** -- Value of materials on-site but not yet installed.
- **Total completed** -- Cumulative total including this period.
- **Percentage complete** -- Auto-calculated from total completed vs. scheduled value.
- **Balance to finish** -- Remaining amount on this line.
- **Retainage** -- Amount held back per your retainage percentage.

## Filling in progress

1. Open your pay app and go to the **Schedule of Values** tab.
2. For each line item, enter either a dollar amount or percentage complete for this period.
3. Previous application amounts are read-only and carry forward automatically.
4. Review the totals at the bottom.

## Tips

- If your bid was converted to a project, you can import line items directly into the SOV.
- Keep descriptions consistent with your contract to avoid disputes.
- Materials stored should reflect inventory on-site that has not been installed yet.
