## The SOV table

On a pay app's detail page, the **Schedule of Values** card lists your line items. The columns are:

- **Item** -- the item number.
- **Description** -- what the line covers.
- **Scheduled Value** -- the contract amount for the line.
- **Previous Apps** -- completed and billed in prior pay apps.
- **This Period** -- completed in the current period.
- **Materials Stored** -- materials on site but not yet installed.
- **Total to Date** -- the sum of previous, this period, and materials stored.

A **Grand Total** row sums every line.

## Add a line item

<Steps>

1. **Add a line.** Click **Add Line Item**.

2. **Describe it.** Enter an **Item Number**, a **Description**, and the **Scheduled Value**.

3. **Report progress.** Enter **This Period** and any **Materials Stored** for the current billing period.

</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
  **Previous Apps** is carried forward from the prior pay app and cannot be edited here. It locks once an earlier pay app exists for the project.
</Aside>

## How the SOV is seeded

The SOV is not imported with a button. On the first pay app, BuildWorkPro seeds it from any pending approved change orders, and the **Original Contract Sum** prefills from the project's accepted bid. Each later pay app carries forward the lines and previous-period totals from the one before it. Add or adjust lines with **Add Line Item** as the job evolves.
