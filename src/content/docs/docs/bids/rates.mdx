The **Rates** tab controls how your costs become a sell price. It sets the margin, commission, and overhead applied to the bid.

![The Rates tab of a bid, showing the Rate Settings card, Display Options, and the Live Preview panel](/docs-screenshots/bids/bid-rates-tab.png)

## Rate settings

Open the **Rates** tab. The **Rate Settings** card has three percentages, each expressed as a percentage of the selling price:

- **Margin** -- your profit margin.
- **Commission** -- sales commission.
- **Company OH** -- company overhead for general business expenses.

Changes save automatically. Click **Reset to Defaults** to return to your organization's default rates.

<Aside type="note">
  Tax is not set here. Each cost item carries its own **Buy Tax** and **Sell Tax** -- see [line items](/docs/bids/line-items/).
</Aside>

## Display options

The **Display Options** card controls how the bid document reads:

- **Show Cost Item Details** -- show material and labor cost items under each line item on the bid.
- **Round to whole dollars** -- round displayed amounts to whole dollars.

## Set defaults for new bids

To pre-fill these rates on every new bid, set your organization defaults.

<Steps>

1. **Open workspace settings.** Go to **Settings -> Workspace**.

2. **Edit Default Bid Rates.** In the **Default Bid Rates** card, set **Margin**, **Commission**, and **Company OH**. New bids start from these values.

</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Company Admin to change workspace defaults. Per-bid rates can be adjusted by anyone who can edit the bid.
</Aside>
