Proper rate configuration keeps your bids profitable. BuildWorkPro lets you set markup, overhead, labor rates, and tax at the bid level.

## Margin and overhead

- **Markup percentage** — applied on top of your direct costs. A 15% markup on $10,000 in costs produces an $11,500 bid price.
- **Overhead** — a separate percentage that covers general business expenses such as insurance, office rent, and vehicles.

Apply these globally to the whole bid, or override them on individual line items.

## Labor rates

Set hourly rates for different labor classifications:

- **Journeyman** — your standard skilled-worker rate.
- **Apprentice** — reduced rate for apprentice-level labor.
- **Foreman** — higher rate for supervisory crew.

Labor rates factor into line items tagged with the **Labor** mark. When you update a rate, every associated line item recalculates automatically.

## Tax

<Steps>

1. Set a **tax rate** percentage.

2. Choose which line items are taxable — materials are typically taxable, labor often is not.

3. Tax calculates on the subtotal of taxable items.

</Steps>

## Review the bid summary

The bid summary shows your total broken down by subtotal, markup, overhead, tax, and grand total. Review it before sending to confirm your numbers.
