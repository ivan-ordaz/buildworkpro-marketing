Once your bid is complete, you can send it directly to your client and track their response.

## Send to client

1. Open the bid and review all line items, rates, and terms.
2. Click **Send Bid**.
3. Confirm the recipient email address. You can add CC recipients if needed.
4. Add a personal message to accompany the proposal.
5. Click **Send**.

The client receives a professional PDF proposal attached to your email. The bid status changes from **Draft** to **Sent**.

## Track bid status

Bids move through these statuses:

- **Draft** -- Still being prepared.
- **Sent** -- Delivered to the client.
- **Viewed** -- The client opened the proposal.
- **Accepted** -- The client agreed to the terms.
- **Rejected** -- The client declined.
- **Expired** -- Past the expiration date with no response.

You can see all bid statuses at a glance from the Bids list page.

## Convert to project

When a client accepts your bid:

1. Open the accepted bid.
2. Click **Convert to Project**.
3. Review the project details -- name, client, and contract value carry over automatically.
4. Click **Create Project**.

The new project is linked to the original bid. Line items can be used to seed your schedule of values for pay applications.
