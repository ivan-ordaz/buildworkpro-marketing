The **Project Costs** tab holds bid-level costs -- such as mobilization, a dumpster, or a permit -- that apply to the whole job rather than to one line. BuildWorkPro spreads them across your line items' sell prices.

## Add a project cost

<Steps>

1. **Open the Project Costs tab.** Open your bid and click **Project Costs**.

2. **Add a cost.** Click **Add Cost**, pick a **Product**, add a **Description**, and enter a **Rate** in dollars.

</Steps>

The table lists each cost's **Product**, **Description**, **Rate**, and **Per Unit** amount.

## How costs are spread

Project costs are loaded into the sell price of your line items, not shown as a separate charge. The summary box reports:

- **Total Project Costs** -- the sum of every project cost.
- **Per unit (avg)** -- the total divided by your total line-item quantity.

<Aside type="note">
  Project costs only spread once you have line items. Add at least one [line item](/docs/bids/line-items/) to see how they affect your loaded sell prices.
</Aside>
