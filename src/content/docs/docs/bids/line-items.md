Line items are the core of your bid. They break down the work into priced components that your client can review.

## Adding line items

1. Open your bid and go to the **Line Items** tab.
2. Click **Add Line Item**.
3. Enter a description, quantity, unit (each, LF, SF, etc.), and unit price.
4. The extended price calculates automatically.

## Using marks

Marks let you tag line items for organization and filtering. Common marks include:

- **Labor** -- Installation and crew time.
- **Material** -- Products and supplies.
- **Equipment** -- Rental or owned tool costs.

You can create custom marks that match how your company categorizes work.

## Sublines

Break complex items into sublines for detailed pricing. For example, a "Panel Installation" line item might have sublines for the panel itself, breakers, conduit, and wire. The parent line item totals up all subline values.

## Product catalog

Save time by pulling items from your product catalog.

1. Click **Add from Catalog** on any line item.
2. Search or browse your saved products.
3. Select an item -- its description, unit, and price auto-fill.

Keep your catalog updated in **Settings > Products** so your team always bids with current pricing. This reduces errors and speeds up the estimating process.
