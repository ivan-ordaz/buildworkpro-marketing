## Start a new bid

<Steps>

1. **Open the Bids list.** Click **Bids** in the sidebar.

2. **Create the bid.** Click **New Bid**. A new bid opens on its **Overview** tab with a bid number assigned automatically.

</Steps>

A bid has seven tabs: **Overview**, **Estimate**, **Project Costs**, **Rates**, **Comments**, **Documents**, and **Activity**. The Comments, Documents, and Activity tabs unlock once you save the bid.

## Fill in the details

On the **Overview** tab, complete the **Bid Details** card:

- **Bid Name** -- a descriptive name such as "Marina Bay HVAC Retrofit". It carries into the project if the bid converts.
- **Customer** -- search for an existing contact or create one on the spot.
- **Related Lead** -- optionally link the lead this bid came from.
- **Due Date** -- the GC submission deadline.
- **Valid Until** -- the date your bid pricing expires.
- **Discount** -- an optional fixed (**$ Fixed**) or percentage (**% Percent**) discount applied to the bid subtotal.
- **Site Address** -- the job site location.
- **Payment Terms** and **Terms & Conditions** -- free text, each with a picker to apply a saved clause.

<Aside type="note">
  **Status** is set to Draft and stays locked while the bid is new. You change it after the bid is saved -- see [sending a bid](/docs/bids/sending/).
</Aside>

## Save the bid

Click **Save**. The bid is created in **Draft** status and the remaining tabs unlock. From here you can:

- [Add line items](/docs/bids/line-items/) to build the estimate.
- [Set your rates](/docs/bids/rates/) for margin and overhead.
- [Add project costs](/docs/bids/project-costs/) that load into every line.
- [Send the bid](/docs/bids/sending/) when it is ready.

BuildWorkPro tracks unsaved changes -- the **Save** button appears whenever you have edits, and you are warned before leaving with unsaved work.
