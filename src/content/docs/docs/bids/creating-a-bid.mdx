Bids are how you price work and send proposals to general contractors or property owners. BuildWorkPro gives you a structured way to build accurate estimates.

<Aside type="note" title="Role required">
  **Company Admin**, **Manager**, and **Member** can create and edit bids.
  **Field Crew** and **Viewer** have no access to bids.
</Aside>

## Start a new bid

<Steps>

1. Click **Bids** in the sidebar, then **New Bid**.

2. Enter a descriptive name such as "Oak Valley Office - HVAC Install."

</Steps>

<Aside type="tip">
  You can also press `Cmd+K` and type "new bid" to jump straight into a new
  bid from anywhere in the app.
</Aside>

## Select a contact

Choose the client this bid is for. Pick from your existing contacts or create a new one on the spot. The contact's company name, address, and email populate your proposal automatically.

## Set your terms

Configure the bid details:

- **Bid date** — the date you are submitting the proposal.
- **Expiration date** — how long the bid stays valid (commonly 30, 60, or 90 days).
- **Scope of work** — a text description of what is included.
- **Exclusions** — clearly state what is not covered.
- **Payment terms** — Net 30, progress billing, or custom terms.
- **Notes** — any additional conditions or clarifications.

## Save and continue

Click **Save** to create the bid in **Draft** status. From here you can:

- [Add line items](/docs/bids/line-items/) to detail your pricing.
- [Configure rates and markup](/docs/bids/rates/).
- [Send the bid](/docs/bids/sending/) when you are ready.

<Aside type="note" title="Saving your work">
  Bids use **manual save**. The **Save** button appears whenever you have
  unsaved edits, and BuildWorkPro warns you before you leave the page with
  unsaved work. Nothing is committed until you click **Save**.
</Aside>
