Once your bid is complete, you can send it directly to your client and track their response.

## Send to the client

<Steps>

1. Open the bid and review the line items, rates, and terms.

2. Click **Send Bid**.

3. Confirm the recipient email address. Add CC recipients if needed.

4. Add a personal message to accompany the proposal.

5. Click **Send**.

</Steps>

The client receives a professional PDF proposal attached to your email. The bid status changes from **Draft** to **Sent**.

![The Send Bid dialog](/docs-screenshots/bids/bid-send-dialog.png)

{/* SCREENSHOT NEEDED: the Send Bid dialog showing the recipient email, CC field, and personal message box. */}

<Aside type="note" title="Role required">
  Sending bids requires **Company Admin** or **Manager**. **Member** can build
  and edit bids but cannot send them.
</Aside>

## Track the bid status

Bids move through these statuses:

- **Draft** — still being prepared.
- **Sent** — delivered to the client.
- **Accepted** — the client agreed to the terms.
- **Rejected** — the client declined.
- **Expired** — past the expiration date with no response.

You can see every bid's status at a glance on the Bids list page.

![The Bids list showing a mix of status badges](/docs-screenshots/bids/bid-status-list.png)

{/* SCREENSHOT NEEDED: the Bids list page showing a mix of Draft, Sent, Accepted, Rejected, and Expired status badges. */}

## Convert an accepted bid to a project

When a client accepts your bid:

<Steps>

1. Open the accepted bid.

2. Click **Convert to Project**.

3. Review the project details — name, client, and contract value carry over automatically.

4. Click **Create Project**.

</Steps>

![The Convert to Project confirmation](/docs-screenshots/bids/bid-convert-to-project.png)

{/* SCREENSHOT NEEDED: the Convert to Project confirmation showing the carried-over project name, client, and contract value. */}

The new project is linked to the original bid, and the bid's line items can seed your schedule of values for [pay applications](/docs/pay-apps/creating/).
