Bids are how you price work and send proposals to general contractors or property owners. BuildWorkPro gives you a structured way to build accurate estimates.

## Start a new bid

1. Click **Bids** in the sidebar, then **New Bid**.
2. Enter a descriptive name like "Oak Valley Office - HVAC Install."

## Select a contact

Choose the client this bid is for. You can pick from your existing contacts or create a new one on the spot. The contact's company name, address, and email will populate your proposal automatically.

## Set your terms

Configure the bid details:

- **Bid date** -- The date you are submitting the proposal.
- **Expiration date** -- How long the bid stays valid (commonly 30, 60, or 90 days).
- **Scope of work** -- A text description of what is included.
- **Exclusions** -- Clearly state what is not covered.
- **Payment terms** -- Net 30, progress billing, or custom terms.
- **Notes** -- Any additional conditions or clarifications.

## Save and continue

Click **Save** to create the bid in draft status. From here you can:

- [Add line items](/docs/bids/line-items) to detail your pricing.
- [Configure rates and markup](/docs/bids/rates).
- [Send the bid](/docs/bids/sending) when you are ready.

Bids auto-save as you work, so you will not lose progress.
