BuildWorkPro has two kinds of reusable content for bids: **estimate templates** that save a whole line-item structure, and saved **payment terms and conditions** clauses.

## Estimate templates

Save the line items and cost items of a bid as a template, then load them into a new bid to skip re-entry.

<Steps>

1. **Save a template.** On the **Estimate** tab, click **Save Template**. In the **Save as Template** dialog, enter a **Template Name** and an optional **Description**, then click **Save Template**.

2. **Load a template.** On a new bid's **Estimate** tab, click **Load Template**, then click **Load** next to the template you want. Its marks, products, and cost items are restored.

</Steps>

<Aside type="tip">
  Build a template from a typical job -- a standard service call or a common system install -- so most new bids start 80% complete.
</Aside>

## Payment terms and conditions

Reusable payment terms and terms-and-conditions clauses are managed separately.

<Steps>

1. **Open the templates settings.** Go to **Settings -> Bid Templates**.

2. **Manage your clauses.** Save reusable **Payment Terms** and **Terms & Conditions** text.

</Steps>

Apply a saved clause to a bid from the **Payment Terms** and **Terms & Conditions** fields on the bid's [Overview tab](/docs/bids/creating-a-bid/).
