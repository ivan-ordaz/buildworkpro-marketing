Every saved bid can be previewed and downloaded as a client-ready PDF, and duplicated to reuse as the basis for a new bid.

## Preview and download the PDF

The bid's action bar has two PDF actions:

- **Preview** -- opens the bid PDF in a preview dialog so you can check it before sending.
- **PDF** -- downloads the bid as `Bid-<number>.pdf`.

The same PDF attaches automatically when you [send the bid](/docs/bids/sending/).

## Duplicate a bid

Reuse a finished bid instead of starting over.

<Steps>

1. **Open the source bid.** Open the bid you want to copy.

2. **Duplicate it.** Click **Duplicate**. BuildWorkPro creates a new bid from the original, with its own bid number in **Draft** status.

3. **Adjust the copy.** Update the customer, dates, and line items for the new job.

</Steps>

<Aside type="tip">
  Duplication is handy for repeat clients or near-identical scopes. For estimates you reuse constantly, an [estimate template](/docs/bids/templates/) is cleaner.
</Aside>
