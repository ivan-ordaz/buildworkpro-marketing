When you delete a contact, lead, bid, project, product, or pay app, BuildWorkPro soft-deletes it -- the record is hidden but not destroyed. The **Deleted Records** tab lets you bring it back.

![Deleted Records manager with the record type dropdown set to Contacts](/docs-screenshots/settings/deleted-records.png)

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Company Admin. Deleted Records is only available to Company Admins.
</Aside>

## Restore a record

<Steps>

1. **Open Deleted Records.** Go to **Settings -> Deleted Records**.

2. **Pick a type.** Choose the record type from the dropdown -- Contacts, Leads, Bids, Projects, Products, or Pay Apps. The list shows what has been deleted.

3. **Restore.** Click the restore icon on a row, or select several and click **Restore Selected** to bring them all back at once.

</Steps>

A restored record returns to its module with its data intact.

<Aside type="tip">
  Soft delete is why a removed record can be recovered. Deleting from any module sends the record here rather than erasing it.
</Aside>
