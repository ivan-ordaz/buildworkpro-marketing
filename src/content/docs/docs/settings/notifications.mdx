The **Notifications** tab controls the alerts you personally receive. Settings here apply to your account only, and every role can manage them. Each toggle saves on its own -- there is no separate Save button.

## Desktop notifications

A single toggle asks your browser or operating system for permission to show desktop notifications. Click **Enable** and allow the prompt.

## In-app notifications

In-app notifications appear in the bell menu inside BuildWorkPro. Toggle them by category:

- **Sales & CRM** -- Lead Assigned, Bid Accepted, Bid Rejected.
- **Projects & Tasks** -- Task Assigned, Task Due Soon.
- **Collaboration** -- mentions and comments.

## Email notifications

Email notifications are sent to your address. Toggle them by category:

- **Sales** -- Lead Assigned, Bid Accepted, Bid Rejected, Bid Expired.
- **Pay Applications** -- Submitted, Approved, Rejected.
- **Change Orders** -- Submitted, Approved, Rejected, Voided.
- **Projects & Tasks** -- Task Due Soon.
- **Digests** -- Daily and Weekly summaries.

<Aside type="note">
  Mentions, comments, and task assignments are delivered in-app, not by email.
</Aside>
