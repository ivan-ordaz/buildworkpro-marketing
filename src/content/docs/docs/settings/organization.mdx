The **Organization** tab holds your company's identity and is the entry point to team management. Open it from **Settings -> Organization**.

![Organization settings tab showing the Organization Details card and the User Management section](/docs-screenshots/settings/organization-settings.png)

## Organization details

The **Organization Details** card shows:

- **Organization Name** -- your company's name as it appears in the app. Company Admins can edit it.
- **Slug** -- a read-only short identifier for your workspace.

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Company Admin to change the organization name. Other roles see the details but cannot edit them.
</Aside>

## Team management

The same tab contains the **User Management** section, where Company Admins invite teammates, edit roles, and deactivate accounts. See [team and roles](/docs/settings/team-roles/) for the full role matrix and the invite flow.

## Where branding and defaults live

Your logo, accent color, and document layout are not on this tab -- they are on the **Workspace** tab, along with numbering, tax defaults, and bid rates. See [workspace defaults](/docs/settings/workspace-defaults/) to set them.
