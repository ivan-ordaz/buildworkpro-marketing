BuildWorkPro uses role-based access control so each teammate sees and does only what their job requires. Every user has exactly one role.

![User Management list showing each teammate with a role badge — Company Admin, Manager, Member, Field Crew, and Viewer](/docs-screenshots/settings/user-management.png)

## The five roles

- **Company Admin** -- full access to everything, including team management, billing, and all settings. The only role that can hard-delete records, manage billing, and change organization settings.
- **Manager** -- everything a Member can do, plus delete records, approve pay apps and change orders, manage the product catalog, send documents, and review site logs and time.
- **Member** -- view all business data and the team roster; create and edit contacts, leads, bids, projects, pay apps, and change orders; export reports; upload documents. Cannot delete, approve, or manage products.
- **Field Crew** -- for on-site workers. View projects and contacts; create and edit site logs and time entries; view documents. No access to bids, pay apps, change orders, leads, products, reports, billing, or settings.
- **Viewer** -- read-only access to business data. Cannot create, edit, or delete anything, cannot see the team roster, and cannot export reports.

## Manage your team

Team management lives on the **Organization** settings tab.

<Steps>

1. **Open team management.** Go to **Settings -> Organization**. The **User Management** section lists every user with their role and status.

2. **Edit a user.** Click a user to change their name, email, role, or active status, then save.

</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Company Admin. Only Company Admins can invite, edit, or remove users.
</Aside>

## Invite a user

<Steps>

1. **Start an invite.** In **User Management**, click **Invite User**.

2. **Enter details.** Add the person's **Email** and choose a **Role**.

3. **Send.** Click **Send Invitation**. The user receives an email link and sets their own password.

</Steps>

Pending invitations appear in their own section, where you can resend or revoke them.
