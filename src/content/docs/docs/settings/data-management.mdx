The **Data Management** tab imports records from CSV files and exports your data back out. Open it from **Settings -> Data Management**.

{/* TODO(media): screenshot of the Data Management import and export cards, per docs-rewrite-plan Batch 5. */}

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Company Admin. The Data Management tab is only available to Company Admins.
</Aside>

## Import data

The **Import Data** card runs guided CSV wizards for:

- **Contacts**
- **Products**
- **Leads**
- **Projects**

<Steps>

1. **Pick a type.** Choose what you are importing and download the sample CSV if you need the column layout.

2. **Upload your file.** Select your CSV and map its columns to BuildWorkPro fields.

3. **Import.** Review the preview and run the import.

</Steps>

<Aside type="tip">
  For importing vendor price lists into a bid, use the [CSV Import Templates](/docs/documents/csv-import-templates/) feature instead -- it maps spreadsheet columns to bid line items.
</Aside>

## Export data

The **Export Data** card downloads your records as CSV. Filter by entity, search, and date range, then click **Download CSV** to export just the rows you need.
