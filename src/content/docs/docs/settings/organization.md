Organization settings control how your company appears in proposals, emails, and throughout the platform. Only Admins can change these settings.

## Company profile

Go to **Settings > Organization** to update your company details:

- **Company name** -- Your legal or trade business name.
- **Phone number** -- Primary business phone.
- **Email** -- Main contact email address.
- **Address** -- Your office or mailing address.
- **Website** -- Your company's website URL.

## Branding

Customize how your proposals and documents look:

- **Logo** -- Upload your company logo. It appears on bids, pay apps, and PDF exports.
- **Brand color** -- Set a primary color that matches your brand identity.

Your logo and color carry through to client-facing documents, giving proposals a professional appearance.

## Default settings

Configure defaults that apply across your organization:

- **Default payment terms** -- Set standard terms (Net 30, Net 60, etc.) that auto-fill on new bids.
- **Default retainage** -- Set a standard retainage percentage for pay apps.
- **Default tax rate** -- Your standard tax rate for bids.
- **Fiscal year start** -- Used for financial reporting periods.

## License and trade info

- **License number** -- Your contractor license for inclusion on proposals.
- **Trade/specialty** -- Your primary trade classification.
- **Insurance info** -- Policy details for reference.

Keeping this information current ensures your documents are always accurate and professional.
