The **Workspace** tab holds the defaults that apply across your organization -- numbering, branding, pipeline stages, product categories, and bid defaults. Open it from **Settings -> Workspace**.

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Company Admin. Non-admins see only the Application Preferences card; the configuration sub-tabs below require settings-edit access.
</Aside>

The tab is split into sub-tabs:

## General

Application preferences (restart the onboarding tour, view keyboard shortcuts) and **Bid & Project Numbering** -- set a **Prefix** and **Starting number** for bids and projects so documents number the way your office expects.

## Pipeline

**Lead Pipeline Stages** -- add, rename, reorder, and color your sales stages, and mark which stages count as Won or Lost. These stages drive the [CRM pipeline board](/docs/crm/pipeline/).

## Categories

**Product Categories** -- create and organize the categories used to group your [product catalog](/docs/products/catalog/), including parent and child categories.

## Bid Defaults

Defaults applied to every new bid:

- **Bid rates** -- default labor, markup, and overhead rates.
- **Tax defaults** -- default purchase and sales tax rates.
- **Currency display** -- whole-dollar rounding.
- **Bid validity and terms** -- default validity in days, estimator SLA, and default terms and conditions text.

## Email Templates

Reusable email templates for bids, change orders, and pay apps, used by the **Send to Customer** flows.

## Project Templates

Reusable phase-and-task structures for new projects. See [project templates](/docs/projects/templates/) for how to build and apply them.

## Branding

**Company Branding** controls how your documents look:

- **Layout** -- Clean, Classic, or Bold.
- **Company Logo** -- upload a PNG, JPG, SVG, or WebP (up to 2 MB).
- **Company Name**, **Tagline**, and **Footer**.
- **Accent Color** -- choose a preset or enter a hex value.
- **Font** -- Sans or Serif.

A live preview shows your changes before you save.

<Aside type="tip">
  Reusable **Payment Terms** and **Terms & Conditions** clause libraries live on the separate **Bid Templates** tab, available to Company Admins and Managers.
</Aside>
