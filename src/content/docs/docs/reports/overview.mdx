The **Reports** module turns your data into KPI cards and charts. Open it from **Reports** in the sidebar. A period selector at the top right filters every report to a date range, and you can export what you see.

![The Reports page showing KPI cards and charts](/docs-screenshots/reports/reports.png)

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Company Admin, Manager, Member, or Viewer can view reports. Field Crew has no access. Exporting requires Member or above.
</Aside>

## Report tabs

Reports are split into four tabs, each with KPI cards and two charts:

- **Pipeline** -- Total Leads, Pipeline Value, Weighted Pipeline, and Won Deals, with pipeline-by-stage and win/loss charts.
- **Bids** -- Total Bids, Conversion Rate, Total Quoted, and Accepted Value, with bids-by-status charts.
- **Projects** -- Total Projects, In Progress, Completed, and Total Value, with status and value-by-type charts.
- **Activity** -- New Contacts and Total Activities, with activity-by-type and new-contacts-by-type charts.

## Filter by period

Use the period selector to scope every report -- this month, year to date, and other ranges. The KPI cards and charts recalculate for the selected window.

## Export

Open the **Export** menu to download the current report as a CSV, or print it to save as a PDF.
