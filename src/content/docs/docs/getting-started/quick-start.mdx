Get up and running with BuildWorkPro in a few minutes. This guide covers account creation, organization setup, and inviting your first team members.

## Create your account

<Steps>

1. Go to the BuildWorkPro sign-up page.

2. Enter your name, email, and a password.

3. Verify your email address through the confirmation link you receive.

</Steps>

![The BuildWorkPro sign-up form](/docs-screenshots/getting-started/signup-form.png)

{/* SCREENSHOT NEEDED: the public sign-up page showing the name, email, and password fields. */}

## Create your organization

After signing in for the first time, you are prompted to create your organization. Your organization is your workspace — all data lives inside it and is isolated from every other company on the platform.

<Steps>

1. Enter your company name, phone number, and address.

2. Choose your industry or trade specialty.

3. Upload your company logo. This is optional, but recommended — your logo appears on proposals and PDF exports.

</Steps>

![The create-organization form](/docs-screenshots/getting-started/org-setup-form.png)

{/* SCREENSHOT NEEDED: the "Create your organization" form with company name, phone, address, trade dropdown, and logo upload. */}

## Invite your team

<Steps>

1. Go to **Settings > Team**.

2. Click **Invite Member** and enter the person's email address.

3. Assign a role: **Company Admin**, **Manager**, **Member**, **Field Crew**, or **Viewer**.

4. Send the invitation. The invited user receives an email with a link to join. They create their own password and are added to your organization automatically.

</Steps>

![The Invite Member dialog with the role dropdown](/docs-screenshots/getting-started/team-invite-dialog.png)

{/* SCREENSHOT NEEDED: Settings > Team with the Invite Member dialog open, role dropdown expanded showing all five roles. */}

<Aside type="note" title="Role required">
  Only **Company Admin** can invite team members and change roles. **Manager**,
  **Member**, **Field Crew**, and **Viewer** cannot manage the team roster.
</Aside>

<Aside type="tip">
  Not sure which role to give someone? See [Team and
  Roles](/docs/settings/team-roles/) for what each role can and cannot do.
</Aside>
