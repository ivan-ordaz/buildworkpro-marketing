BuildWorkPro has two ways to get anywhere: the sidebar for browsing and the command palette for jumping straight to a record.

![The command palette open over the dashboard with "bid" typed, listing matching bids with their totals](/docs-screenshots/getting-started/command-palette-search.png)

## The sidebar

The sidebar is a dark navy icon rail down the left edge. Click an icon to open its flyout menu. The main destinations are:

- **Dashboard** -- your home screen.
- **Sales** -- Contacts, Pipeline, and Bids.
- **Projects** -- All Projects, Change Orders, Pay Apps, Invoices, Site Logs, and Time Tracking.
- **Calendar** -- the app-wide calendar.
- **Documents** -- your document library.
- **Products** -- your product catalog.
- **Reports** -- pipeline, bid, project, and activity reports.
- **Activity** -- the org-wide activity history.
- **Settings** -- account and company settings, at the bottom of the rail.

What you see depends on your role. Field Crew, for example, does not see Bids or billing.

<Aside type="note">
  Notifications are not in the sidebar. Open them from the bell icon in the top header.
</Aside>

On a phone, the sidebar collapses to a bottom bar with Dashboard, Pipeline, Projects, Site Logs, and Time, plus a **More** button for everything else.

## The command palette

The command palette is the fastest way to find a specific record. Press <kbd>Cmd</kbd>+<kbd>K</kbd> on a Mac or <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>K</kbd> on Windows or Linux. You can also click the search box in the header.

Start typing and the palette searches across your whole workspace:

Contacts, Leads, Projects, Bids, Change Orders, Pay Apps, Invoices, Site Logs, Documents, Products, Tasks, and CSV Import Templates.

Select a result to jump straight to it. Before you type, the palette also lists quick actions and your recent searches.

<Aside type="tip">
  Narrow a search to one record type with a `type:` prefix. For example, `type:bid oak valley` searches only bids. Aliases include `type:contact`, `type:project`, `type:co` for change orders, and `type:payapp` for pay apps.
</Aside>

## Keyboard shortcuts

| Shortcut | Action |
| --- | --- |
| <kbd>Cmd</kbd>+<kbd>K</kbd> / <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>K</kbd> | Open the command palette |
| <kbd>Cmd</kbd>+<kbd>Enter</kbd> / <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>Enter</kbd> | Save the open form |
| <kbd>Esc</kbd> | Close a dialog, flyout, or inline editor |
