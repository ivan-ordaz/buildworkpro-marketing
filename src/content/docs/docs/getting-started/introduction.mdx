BuildWorkPro is a construction management platform built for subcontractors. It brings bidding, project management, billing, and field operations into one place, so you stop juggling spreadsheets and disconnected tools.

## Who it is for

BuildWorkPro is designed for specialty trade contractors: electrical, plumbing, HVAC, concrete, framing, and other subcontractor businesses. Whether you run a five-person crew or a 200-person operation, the platform scales with you.

## What you can do

- **Bidding** — Build detailed estimates with line items, markup, labor rates, and material costs, then send professional proposals to clients.
- **Project management** — Track work across phases and tasks. Use board, list, grid, or Gantt views to stay on schedule.
- **Pay applications** — Create AIA-style pay apps with a schedule of values. Submit them, track approval, and get paid faster.
- **Change orders** — Document scope changes, route them for approval, and update contract values automatically.
- **Field tools** — Capture daily site logs, weather, crew hours, and photo documentation.
- **CRM** — Manage contacts, track leads through your pipeline, and convert won deals into projects.
- **Team management** — Role-based access so each person sees exactly what they need.

## How your workspace is organized

BuildWorkPro is a multi-tenant platform. Each company gets its own **organization** — an isolated workspace with separate data, users, and billing. Everything you create (contacts, bids, projects, pay apps) lives inside your organization and is never visible to other companies.

Within your organization, every team member has one of five **roles** that controls what they can see and do:

- **Company Admin** — full access, including billing, team management, and record deletion.
- **Manager** — elevated access; can delete records, send quotes, manage products, and approve pay apps.
- **Member** — create and edit records assigned to them; can export reports.
- **Field Crew** — restricted to projects, site logs, and time tracking for on-site work.
- **Viewer** — read-only access.

See [Team and Roles](/docs/settings/team-roles/) for the full breakdown.

## Finding things fast

Press `Cmd+K` (`Ctrl+K` on Windows) anywhere in the app to open the command palette. Start typing the name of a contact, bid, project, or page, and jump straight to it without navigating the sidebar.
