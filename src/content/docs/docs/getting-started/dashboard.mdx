The dashboard is your home screen. It opens whenever you sign in and gives you a snapshot of your pipeline, projects, and recent activity. A time-based greeting at the top welcomes you by name.

![The BuildWorkPro dashboard showing pipeline value, win rate, active projects, and revenue history](/docs-screenshots/getting-started/dashboard.png)

## KPI cards

A row of stat cards across the top tracks your key numbers. By default you see four:

- **Pipeline Value** -- the total value of open leads.
- **Win Rate** -- the share of decided bids you have won.
- **Active Projects** -- projects currently in progress.
- **Pending Bids** -- bids still in draft or sent.

Each card links to a filtered list, so clicking **Active Projects** opens your in-progress projects. Other available cards include Contract Value, Planning, Overdue, Completed, Won Leads (est.), and Contacts.

<Aside type="tip">
  The cards are yours to arrange. Click **Customize** to show or hide cards and drag them into the order you want.
</Aside>

## Quick actions

Below the cards, quick-action buttons start the most common tasks without leaving the dashboard:

- **Add Contact** -- opens the new-contact form.
- **Add Lead** -- opens the new-lead form.
- **Create Bid** -- starts a new bid.
- **New Project** -- opens the new-project form.

You only see the actions your role allows. A Viewer, who cannot create records, sees none of them.

## Widgets

The rest of the dashboard is a grid of widgets you can rearrange from the **Customize** panel. They include:

- **Revenue History** -- revenue over time.
- **This Week Summary** -- what is due and happening this week.
- **Pipeline & Bids** -- open pipeline and bid activity.
- **Project Status** -- a breakdown of projects by status.
- **Win / Loss** -- decided bids, won versus lost.
- **Upcoming Deadlines**, **At Risk**, **Project Map**, **Site Log Activity**, and **Projects by Type**.

## Alerts and getting started

When something needs attention -- an overdue project, a stale lead, or a bid that needs follow-up -- a banner appears at the top with a link straight to it.

If your workspace is brand new, a **Getting Started** card appears above your widgets and points you to your first contact, lead, project, or bid. It disappears once you have data.
