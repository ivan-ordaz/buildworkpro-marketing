Time tracking logs the hours your crew works on each project, so labor cost rolls into job costing. **Time Tracking** is a top-level module -- open it from the sidebar to see entries across every project. Each project also has its own **Time** tab.

## Log time

<Steps>

1. **Start an entry.** Click **Log Time**.

2. **Pick the project and date.** Choose the **Project** and the **Date**.

3. **Enter hours.** Enter the **Hours** worked (in quarter-hour steps), optionally pick a **Task**, and add **Notes**.

4. **Save.** Click **Log Time**.

</Steps>

Hours are entered manually. The page header totals the hours and cost for the selected period.

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Company Admin, Manager, Member, or Field Crew can log and edit time. Viewer is read-only.
</Aside>

## How cost is calculated

You do not enter a rate on the time entry. BuildWorkPro applies the labor rate set for the user on that project and shows the computed cost -- for example "$45/hr x 8 hrs". A manager can review entries before they are finalized.

## Browse and filter

Entries are grouped by day, each showing the user, project, hours, cost, and review status. Filter by date range, project, or reviewed status.
