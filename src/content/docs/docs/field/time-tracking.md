Time tracking lets you log hours worked by your crew on each project. This feeds into job costing so you can see whether projects are on budget.

## Logging hours

1. Open your project and go to the **Time Tracking** section.
2. Click **Add Time Entry**.
3. Fill in:
   - **Worker** -- Select the team member.
   - **Date** -- When the work was performed.
   - **Hours** -- Regular hours worked.
   - **Overtime hours** -- Any hours beyond standard (typically over 8/day or 40/week).
   - **Description** -- What they worked on.

## Labor rates

Each time entry is costed using labor rates configured in your settings:

- **Standard rate** -- The base hourly cost for each labor classification.
- **Overtime rate** -- Typically 1.5x the standard rate.
- **Burden rate** -- Additional cost for taxes, insurance, and benefits loaded on top of the base rate.

Labor costs calculate automatically based on hours entered and the applicable rates.

## Reviewing time

The time tracking view shows:

- Total hours by worker, day, or week.
- Labor cost breakdown by project or phase.
- Comparison of budgeted vs. actual labor hours.

## Tips

- Enter time daily to keep records accurate.
- Use the description field to note specific tasks -- this helps when reviewing job costs later.
- Managers can review and approve time entries before they are finalized.
