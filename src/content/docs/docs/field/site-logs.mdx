Site logs are daily records of what happened on a job. They document progress, issues, and visits for project history and dispute resolution. **Site Logs** is a top-level module -- open it from the sidebar to see logs across every project. Each project also has its own **Site Logs** tab showing just that project's entries.

![The Site Logs list showing date, title, tags, and reviewed status](/docs-screenshots/field/site-logs-list.png)

## Create a site log

<Steps>

1. **Start a new log.** Click **New Log Entry**.

2. **Pick the project and date.** Choose the **Project** and the **Date** (defaults to today).

3. **Add the details.** Enter **Personnel on Site**, an optional **Title**, any **Tags**, and the **Notes**.

4. **Create.** Click **Create Log Entry**.

</Steps>

Tag a log to classify it: Progress, Issue, Delay, Safety, Delivery, Inspection, or General.

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Company Admin, Manager, Member, or Field Crew can create and edit site logs. Field Crew on-site workers are expected users of this module. Viewer is read-only.
</Aside>

## Add more on the detail page

Open a log to fill in the rest:

- **Work Performed**, **Materials Received**, **Visitors on Site**, and **Issues**.
- **Photos** -- attach images from the job site.
- **Mark Reviewed** -- a manager marks the log reviewed once checked.

The log also has its own Comments, Documents, and Activity.

## Browse and filter

The list has a **Timeline** and a **Table** view. Filter by project, tag, or reviewed status, or search by title and notes.
