The **Documents** module is one place to see every file in your workspace. Each document is attached to a record -- a project, bid, contact, lead, pay app, change order, site log, task, or phase -- and the library lets you browse and search them all. Open it from **Documents** in the sidebar.

![The Documents library listing files with their module, linked record, and category](/docs-screenshots/documents/documents-list.png)

## Browse and search

The library has two layouts:

- **Card view** -- browse files grouped by the record they belong to.
- **List view** -- a flat table of every file.

The list shows each file's **Name**, **Module**, **Linked To**, **Category**, **Size**, **Uploaded By**, and **Date**. Sort by most columns, search by filename, and filter by **Module** or **Category** to narrow the list.

## Categories

Documents are tagged with a category so you can group them: Contract, Permit, Drawing, Specification, Photo, Invoice, Proposal, Import, or Other. BuildWorkPro guesses a category from the filename when you upload, and you can change it.

## Upload files

You upload files from inside a record -- open a project, bid, or contact, go to its **Documents** tab, and drag files in or click to browse. The upload dialog accepts up to 20 files at a time, up to 100 MB each, and takes images, PDFs, Office documents, and archives.

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Company Admin, Manager, or Member to upload. Company Admin or Manager to delete. Field Crew and Viewer can view and download but not upload or delete.
</Aside>

## Open, download, or remove a file

Each row's menu lets you preview the file, download it, jump to the record it is linked to, or delete it. Deleting is a soft delete with an undo prompt, so a removed file can be recovered.
