Contacts are the people and companies you work with -- customers, general contractors, vendors, and more. Organize them here so the right person is one search away when you build a bid, project, or pay app. Open **Contacts** from the sidebar.

![The Contacts list showing name, type, email, and phone](/docs-screenshots/crm/contacts-list.png)

## Contact types

Every contact has a type that describes the relationship: Customer, General Contractor, Architect, City, HOA, Vendor, Subcontractor, Employee, or Other. Filter the list by type to narrow it.

## Create a contact

<Steps>

1. **Start a new contact.** Click **Add Contact**.

2. **Choose a type.** Pick the **Contact Type**.

3. **Enter details.** Add the company or person's name, **Position / Role**, and contact details. At least one of **Email**, **Phone**, or **Mobile** is required.

4. **Save.** The contact joins your list.

</Steps>

Contacts can be hierarchical -- set a **Parent Company** to nest an employee under the company they work for.

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Company Admin, Manager, or Member to create or edit. Only Company Admin and Manager can delete. Field Crew and Viewer have read-only access.
</Aside>

## Import contacts from CSV

Bulk import is handled in Settings, not on the Contacts list.

<Steps>

1. **Open Data Management.** Go to **Settings -> Data Management**.

2. **Import contacts.** In the **Import Data** card, choose Contacts and download the sample CSV for the column layout.

3. **Upload and map.** Upload your file, map its columns, and run the import.

</Steps>

See [data management](/docs/settings/data-management/) for the full import and export flow. Importing contacts requires Company Admin.
