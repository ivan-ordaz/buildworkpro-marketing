## Views

The page has three layouts, with **Kanban** as the default:

- **Kanban** -- leads as cards in stage columns; drag a card to move it to another stage.
- **Cards** -- a grid of lead cards.
- **Table** -- a row-per-lead table.

## Configurable stages

Pipeline stages are not fixed. A new workspace starts with seven default stages -- New Lead, Contacted, Site Visit Scheduled, Bid Sent, Negotiation, Won, and Lost -- and you can add, rename, reorder, and recolor them.

Manage stages under **Settings -> Workspace -> Pipeline**. Mark stages as Won or Lost there; those cannot be deleted, and the default stage is assigned to new leads. See [workspace defaults](/docs/settings/workspace-defaults/).

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Company Admin, Manager, or Member to create and edit leads. Only Company Admin and Manager can delete. Viewer is read-only. Field Crew has no access to the pipeline.
</Aside>

## Add a lead

<Steps>

1. **Start a new lead.** Click **Add Lead**.

2. **Enter the opportunity.** Name the lead, link a **Contact**, and set the **Pipeline Value**, **Source**, and **Project Type**.

3. **Save.** The lead starts in your default stage.

</Steps>
