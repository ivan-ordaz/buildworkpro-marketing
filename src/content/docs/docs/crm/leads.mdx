A lead is one opportunity in your [sales pipeline](/docs/crm/pipeline/). Click a lead to open its detail page, where you track its value, log activity, and convert it into a bid or a project when it advances.

![A lead detail page on the Overview tab with lead information and the Lead Summary panel](/docs-screenshots/crm/lead-detail.png)

## The lead detail page

A lead opens on its **Overview** tab, with **Timeline**, **Comments**, **Documents**, and **Activity** tabs alongside. The Overview holds:

- **Lead Name** and **Stage**.
- **Contact** -- the person or company the lead is with.
- **Source** and **Project Type**.
- **Pipeline Value**, **Probability (%)**, and **Expected Close Date**.

A summary card shows the **Weighted Value** -- pipeline value multiplied by probability -- alongside related bids and activity.

## Log activity

Click **Log Activity** to record a phone call, email, meeting, or note against the lead. Logged activity appears on the Timeline and feeds the pipeline reports.

## Convert a lead

When a lead advances, turn it into a bid or a project:

<Steps>

1. **Create a bid.** Click **Create Bid** to start a bid pre-linked to the lead, carrying its contact and details over.

2. **Convert to a project.** Click **Convert to Project** to create a project from the lead. The lead moves to the Won stage. This cannot be undone.

</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Creating a bid requires bid-create access; converting to a project requires lead-edit access. Both are available to Company Admin, Manager, and Member. Field Crew has no access to leads, and Viewer is read-only.
</Aside>
