The **Products** module is your catalog of reusable materials, labor, and other items. Add a product once and pull it into bids and change orders instead of retyping prices. Open it from **Products** in the sidebar.

![The Products catalog listing SKU, name, type, cost, and sell price](/docs-screenshots/products/products-list.png)

## The catalog list

The list shows each product's **SKU**, **Name**, **Type**, **Cost**, **Sell** price, and **Manufacturer**. Search by name, and filter by **Type** -- Material, Labor, Kit, or Other -- or by category.

<Aside type="note">
  **Role required:** Company Admin, Manager, Member, or Viewer can view the catalog. Field Crew has no access. Only Company Admins and Managers can create, edit, or delete products.
</Aside>

## Add a product

<Steps>

1. **Start a new product.** Click **Add Product**.

2. **Name and classify it.** Enter a **Name** and choose a **Type** -- Material, Labor, or Other. Leave **SKU** blank to auto-generate one, or set your own.

3. **Set prices.** Enter the **Cost Price** and **Sell Price**, and the **Purchase Tax** and **Sales Tax** percentages (prefilled from your tax defaults).

4. **Add details.** Set the **Unit of Measure**, an internal and a sales description, and optionally a **Manufacturer** and manufacturer code.

5. **Save.** The product joins your catalog.

</Steps>

## Categories

Products are grouped by category for filtering. Categories are managed under **Settings -> Workspace -> Categories**, where you can create parent and child categories. See [workspace defaults](/docs/settings/workspace-defaults/).

## Delete and restore

Deleting a product is a soft delete with an undo prompt. A Company Admin can restore a deleted product from [Settings -> Deleted Records](/docs/settings/deleted-records/).
